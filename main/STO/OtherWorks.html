Archaeological Internship in Turkey T-Shirt Design

On the front, I used the logo for Antiochia ad Cragum. The back is one of the mosaics found at the site; I did motify the black portion to make part of the design resemble bones. "Kazi Ekibi" is Turkish for excavation team.

Asian Conversation T-Shirt Design

Towards the end of my year long Asian Conversation program, a friend and I decided to create T-shirts for our cohort. The bubble tea and dragon was a cute concept that I decided to try out and ended up being the design. In the corner is Peppa Pig, which I thought was a cool way to connect the "Year of the Pig" and my own signature into the design. Here are the designs and what it looked like on Custom Ink shirts!

Asian American Student Union (ASU) T-Shirt Design

For the ASU T-shirt design, I thought about how food connects across different cultures. So I asked some of our members and friends, what are some of their favorite dishes. Here are the designs and what it looked like on Custom Ink shirts!
