What are Toilet Talks?

Print resources created by the Wellness Center on various topics. These posters go up in various bathrooms located within one of the main school buildings, Buntrock Commons. The topics that are discussed fall within the nine areas of wellness: Social, Spiritual, Evironmental, Sexual, Substance, Financial, Physical, Emotional, and Intellectual.
