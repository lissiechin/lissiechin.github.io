Asian Conversation T-Shirt Design

Towards the end of my year long Asian Conversation program, a friend and I decided to create T-shirts for our cohort. The bubble tea and dragon was a cute concept that I decided to try out and ended up being the design. In the corner, is Peppa Pig which I thought was a cool way to connect the "Year of the Pig" and my own signature into the design.
