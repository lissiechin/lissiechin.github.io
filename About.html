About Me

Lissie Chin

Hello again!
I am an artist based in my hometown, Pleasant Hill, California. Currently, I am attending St. Olaf College located in Minnesota.
Since I was a little girl, art is a major component in my life, but it all begun with the help and mentorship of my aunt. ... Art became our shared interest; we attended artist workshops and the normal exploration at home. Over time, the imagination, creativity, freedom, and expression aspects within remains one of my favorite things about doing art. Additionally, there is the collaboration component of working with others to create our vision.
